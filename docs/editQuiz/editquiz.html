Quiz Description


Shuffle ?⃝Whether or not the question order should be randomized. Reversable ?⃝Whether or not the questions and answers should be randomly switched (good for Quizlets). Time ?⃝How much time the player is given to answer the question. Penalty ?⃝How many points to deduct for an incorrect answer. Case Sensitive ?⃝Whether or not the answer is case-sensitive. Ignore Characters ?⃝Symbols/characters to ignore in the response (ex. spaces). No seperator required.
